Nightcore

Nightcore music refers to a genre of electronic music that involves speeding up and often pitch-shifting songs, typically taken from other genres such as pop, rock, trance, or hip-hop. The name "nightcore" originated from the Norwegian duo of the same name, who first started remixing songs in this style in 2002. Nightcore music gained popularity on platforms like YouTube and SoundCloud, where users would create and share their own nightcore remixes of popular songs. The genre has a dedicated fanbase and continues to evolve with new remixes and interpretations.
